🌊 How to Play Tidewatch

The Basics

🖱️ Drag towers from the bottom bar onto any sandy (beige) tile to place them.
🛣️ Enemies march along the coral-coloured path. Stop them before they reach the sea at the bottom!
🐚 You have 3 lives. Each enemy that escapes costs 1 life. Lose all 3 and it's game over.
🪙 Earn coins by destroying trash. Spend them on new towers or upgrades.
🖱️ Click a placed tower to upgrade it or sell it for half its value back.
⏩ Use the Send Now button to skip the countdown and start the next wave early.

Towers — drag onto sand tiles

🕸️
Net Launcher 🪙 50 Slows all enemies in range by 50%. Doesn't deal damage but lets other towers clean up.
Upgrades: longer slow duration → bigger range
🐦
Seagull Squad 🪙 75 Fast-firing. Targets the nearest enemy and pecks it for 15 damage.
Upgrades: hits 2 enemies at once → damage increases to 25
🐋
Whale Spout 🪙 100 Slow but powerful. Fires a water blast that splashes for 40 AoE damage in a 1-tile radius.
Upgrades: bigger splash radius → damage increases to 70

Animal Companions — earned from the Clam Roll (unlocks after wave 3)

🐟
Swordfish Place on a sand tile. Charges at the nearest enemy and deals 60 damage, then vanishes. Great for picking off a tough target.
🐙
Octopus Place on a sand tile. Instantly freezes up to 3 nearby enemies for 3 seconds, then vanishes. Perfect before a big attack.
🐡
Pufferfish Place on the path. Acts as a mine — explodes when an enemy steps on it, dealing 50 damage in a 2-tile radius. Then it's gone.
🐢
Sea Turtle Place on the path. Blocks the first enemy that reaches it and shoves it back 2 tiles, buying you precious time. Then it leaves.

Seashell Powerups — tap the icons in the top-right to use

🌊
Tidal WaveSlows every enemy on the path by 60% for 5 seconds.
💰
Coin CurrentDoubles all coin drops for the rest of the current wave.
🫧
Sea FoamInstantly restores 1 lost life.
